ABOUT THIS PRODUCTION

Theatres
Al Hirschfeld Theatre
(Jul 12, 2012 - Aug 04, 2012)
This production is a return engagement of Fela! (Nov 23, 2009 - Jan 02, 2011)
Setting
Fela's Shrine in Lagos, Nigeria. Several months after the death of Funmilayo, Fela's mother.
