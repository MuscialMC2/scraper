ABOUT THIS PRODUCTION

Theatres
Palace Theatre
(Aug 21, 1983 - Nov 15, 1987)
Setting
Summer. St. Tropez, France.
Comments
This production was hoping to transfer to the Mark Hellinger Theatre. Lee Roy Reams and Mace Barrett were slated to take over in the roles of Albin and Georges, respectively, on November 19, 1987. However, the production did not transfer to the Hellinger, and closed November 15, 1987, before Reams and Barrett could start performances.
