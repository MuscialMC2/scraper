ABOUT THIS PRODUCTION

The credits for this production have not yet been completed or verified.
This production was composed of the following shows: H.M.S. Pinafore, Trial by Jury
This production played in repertory with The Mikado, The Pirates of Penzance
Theatres
Mark Hellinger Theatre
(Oct 17, 1949 - Oct 22, 1949)
Description
A comic opera in two acts

Songs for the H. M. S. Pinafore are listed as Acts 1 and 2
