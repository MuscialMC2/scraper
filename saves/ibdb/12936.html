ABOUT THIS PRODUCTION

Theatres
Henry Miller's Theatre
(Sep 20, 2001 - Jan 18, 2004)
Setting
Amenity #9, the poorest, filthiest urinal in town. The good offices of Urine Good Company. A secret hideout.
Comments
Original opening night of September 13, 2001 was postponed due to World Trade Center attacks and subsequent Broadway closing.
