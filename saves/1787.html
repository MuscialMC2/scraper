Majestic Theatre
(Oct 10, 1947 - Jul 10, 1948)
Setting
Joseph Taylor, Jr.'s hometown, his college town and a large city. Starting in 1905 on the day Joseph Taylor, Jr. is born, and following his life to his thirty-fifth year.
