ABOUT THIS PRODUCTION

Theatres
Martin Beck Theatre
(Dec 05, 2002 - Jun 20, 2003)
Al Hirschfeld Theatre
(Jun 21, 2003 - Aug 31, 2003)
Description
Suggested by the life and works of Miguel de Cervantes y Saavedra.
Setting
A prison in Seville, Spain, and in the imagination of Miguel de Cervantes. 1594.
Comments
The Martin Beck Theatre was renamed the Al Hirschfeld Theatre on June 21, 2003, during the run of this production.
