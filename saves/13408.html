ABOUT THIS PRODUCTION

Theatres
New Amsterdam Theatre
(Sep 23, 2002 - Sep 23, 2002)
Description
Based on incidents in the life of Fanny Brice.
Setting
Shortly before and after World War I. Various theatres on New York's lower East Side, in Baltimore, and on Long Island.
Comments
The Second Annual Benefit Concert for The Actors' Fund.
