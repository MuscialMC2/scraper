ABOUT THIS PRODUCTION

Theatres
Alvin Theatre
(Apr 20, 1954 - Aug 07, 1954)
Description
A musical in Two Acts
Setting
The State of Washington between 1900 and 1910.
Comments
Transferred from off-Broadway at the Phoenix Theatre (opened March 11, 1954 for 48 performances). During the off-Broadway run, Geraldine Viti played the role of Mrs. Juniper, Nola Day played the role of Mother Hare, and Larry Chelsi played the role of Patroclus; the rest of the cast was identical to that which opened on Broadway.
